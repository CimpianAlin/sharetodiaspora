Advanced Sharer for diaspora*

Share all teh internetz!

Features

  • Share websites on any diaspora* pod. Popular pods already available on a list.
  • Optionally save your last 3 pods.
  • Optional URL shortening via j.mp.
  • Markdown formatting.

Firefox Social Provider

Add a native Share button to the Share Services of Firefox. Click "Share This Page" from the browser toolbar to open the save dialog. Just click the following button and choose Enable Services.

Add to Firefox

Add-on for Firefox

If you do not want to use Firefox' Social API, diaspora* Advanced Sharer by Jason Robinson adds a sharing button to Mozilla Firefox.

Bookmarklet

Instead of using several bookmarklets for several pods, you can just drag this one: Share on diaspora* to your bookmarks bar.

Blogger

This method will include a diaspora* share button next to Google +1 button. It is necessary to enable the sharing buttons.

  • Enter your dashboard and choose your blog.
  • In section Template, click on Edit HTML. If a warning appears, click Continue.
  • Check Expand widget templates, press Ctrl+F and write sharebuttons.
